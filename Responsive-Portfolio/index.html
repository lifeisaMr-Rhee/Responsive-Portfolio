About Me

Gamer, sports enthusiast, festival hunter and all around genuine human being. Some people like only 1 of those things I mentioned and rarely do they usually like all of them. I feel like this makes me a highly unique person and one that can easily get along with most people no matter what their interest, because I most likely have something in common with them.

Recently my hobby has been music production and just attending as many festivals and events that I have time for. The production is coming along slowly as there's always a learning curve. I've been interested in genres like hip hop and rock most of my life, but recently electronic music has made me really enjoy life to the fullest.
